This example directory contains three final theses that have been
preprocessed to be appropriate as input to the KAS-struct software,
along with the output of the tool.

The three theses are from the University of Ljubljana, Faculty of
Computer Science and Informatics (Univerza v Ljubljani, Fakulteta za
računalništvo in informatiko):

kas-7977468 (Doctoral thesis)
Title: Hitra postavitev prevajalnih sistemov na osnovi pravil za sorodne naravne jezike
Author:	Vičič, Jernej
Supervisor: Kononenko, Igor
Year: 2012
URL: http://eprints.fri.uni-lj.si/1778/

kas-8395574 (MSc thesis)
Title: Preiskovanje pri igrah z nepopolno informacijo na primeru tršeta
Author:	Kafol, Žan
Supervisor: Robnik Šikonja, Marko
Year: 2014
URL: https://repozitorij.uni-lj.si/IzpisGradiva.php?id=29965

kas-8395397 (BSc diploma thesis)
Title: Ogrodje za vizualizacijo prostorskih podatkov
Author:	Tramte, Primož
Supervisor: Robnik Šikonja, Marko
Year: 2014
URL: https://repozitorij.uni-lj.si/IzpisGradiva.php?id=29469
